Collections
Category Collection

Collections are the main way to unlock new Crafting Recipes and Trades in SkyBlock. To progress through a Collection players must gather the item or claim it from its respective Minion.


Note: Items dropped or placed by players, purchased from the Auction House, Bazaar, NPCs or traded with other players don't count towards the items Collection.

The Boss Collection works differently than other Collections. To progress through the Boss Collections you must defeat the Boss which is listed there. Each Boss Collection tier unlocked will reward the player with an item instead of a Crafting Recipe.

Overview

To view your Collections, open the SkyBlock Menu and click the painting icon named "Collection." This will present you with 6 categories, Farming, Mining, Combat, Foraging, Fishing, and Boss.
